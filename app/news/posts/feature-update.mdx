We're excited to announce the release of our **AI Interview Coach** feature, designed to help you ace any interview with confidence.

## What's New?

The AI Interview Coach provides:

- **Personalized Questions**: Get questions tailored to your specific role and industry
- **Real-time Feedback**: Receive instant feedback on your answers
- **Practice Sessions**: Simulate real interview conditions
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your improvement over time

## How It Works

1. **Select Your Role**: Choose the position you're interviewing for
2. **Answer Questions**: Respond to AI-generated interview questions
3. **Get Feedback**: Receive detailed feedback on your responses
4. **Improve**: Use insights to refine your interview skills

## User Feedback

Early users have reported:

- 85% feel more confident in interviews
- 70% saw improvement in their interview performance
- 90% would recommend the feature to others
